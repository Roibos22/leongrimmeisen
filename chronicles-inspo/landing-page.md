## Features (3 cards)

### 🎙️ Just talk

Tap record and say whatever's on your mind. No typing, no blank page, no prompts — built for brains that think faster than they type.

### ✍️ AI writes it down

Every entry gets a title and a summary, automatically. Your words stay your words — nothing is rewritten, judged, or coached.

### 📖 Reads like a book

Your days become a timeline set in serif type, with your own voice a tap away. Synced playback highlights each sentence as it plays.

Recording, transcription, and the AI that writes your entries all happen on your iPhone. The App Store privacy label says "Data Not Collected" because there is literally nothing to collect.
